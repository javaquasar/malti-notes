Shopping and Clothes

This page helps with buying clothes, asking about colours and sizes, understanding prices, and using safe shopping phrases such as nixtieq and kemm jiswa?.

clothes colours sizes prices

Best use

  • Start with one clothing word and one colour.
  • Use Nixtieq... for safe requests.
  • Use Kemm jiswa? for price questions.

Shopping

Shopping Patterns

Nixtieq flokk blu.I would like a blue shirt.
Kemm jiswa dan il-flokk?How much does this shirt cost?
Għandek daqs kbir?Do you have a large size?
Nista' nipprova dan?Can I try this on?
Dialogues

Mini-dialogues

Buying a shirt

A: Nixtieq flokk iswed.A: I would like a black shirt.
B: Liema daqs għandek bżonn?B: What size do you need?
A: Daqs medju, jekk jogħġbok.A: Medium size, please.

Asking the price

A: Kemm jiswa dan iż-żarbun?A: How much does this shoe cost?
B: Jiswa tletin ewro.B: It costs thirty euro.
A: Tajjeb, nieħduh.A: Good, I will take it.
Role Play

Role Play Ideas

Customer

Ask for a colour, a size, and a price. Then decide if you want to buy it.

Shop assistant

Offer another size, another colour, and say the price clearly.

Review

Wrong vs Right

Wrong

Jien tixtieq flokk aħmar.Wrong person and verb form.

Right

Jien nixtieq flokk aħmar.I would like a red shirt.
Phrases

50 Thematic Phrases

Nixtieq flokk aħmar.I would like a red shirt.
Nixtieq flokk blu.I would like a blue shirt.
Nixtieq qalziet iswed.I would like black trousers.
Nixtieq libsa ħadra.I would like a green dress.
Nixtieq ġakketta griża.I would like a grey jacket.
Nixtieq żarbun abjad.I would like a white shoe.
Għandek daqs żgħir?Do you have a small size?
Għandek daqs medju?Do you have a medium size?
Għandek daqs kbir?Do you have a large size?
Dan il-flokk huwa sabiħ.This shirt is beautiful.
Din il-libsa hija sabiħa ħafna.This dress is very beautiful.
Dawn iż-żraben huma komdi.These shoes are comfortable.
Kemm jiswa dan il-flokk?How much does this shirt cost?
Kemm tiswa din il-libsa?How much does this dress cost?
Kemm jiswew dawn iż-żraben?How much do these shoes cost?
Dan jiswa għoxrin ewro.This costs twenty euro.
Din tiswa ħmistax-il ewro.This costs fifteen euro.
Dawn jiswew tletin ewro.These cost thirty euro.
Nista' nipprova dan il-flokk?Can I try on this shirt?
Nista' nipprova din il-libsa?Can I try on this dress?
Nista' nipprova dawn iż-żraben?Can I try on these shoes?
Dan huwa kbir wisq.This is too big.
Dan huwa żgħir wisq.This is too small.
Dan huwa perfett.This is perfect.
Għandek kulur ieħor?Do you have another colour?
Għandek daqs ieħor?Do you have another size?
Nixtieq nara l-kulur blu.I would like to see the blue colour.
Nixtieq nara l-kulur iswed.I would like to see the black colour.
Dan mhux il-kulur favorit tiegħi.This is not my favourite colour.
Inħobb il-kulur aħdar.I like the colour green.
Ma nħobbx il-kulur roża.I do not like the colour pink.
Il-ġakketta hija għalja.The jacket is expensive.
Il-flokk huwa irħis.The shirt is cheap.
Din il-borża hija kbira.This bag is big.
Dik il-borża hija żgħira.That bag is small.
Jien għandi bżonn ġakketta ġdida.I need a new jacket.
Jien għandi bżonn żraben ġodda.I need new shoes.
Ommi trid libsa ġdida.My mother wants a new dress.
Missieri jrid qalziet ġdid.My father wants new trousers.
Hemm skont fuq dawn iż-żraben?Is there a discount on these shoes?
Nista' nħallas bil-karta?Can I pay by card?
Nista' nħallas bi flus kontanti?Can I pay in cash?
Se nieħu dan il-flokk.I will take this shirt.
Se nieħu din il-libsa.I will take this dress.
Se nieħu dawn iż-żraben.I will take these shoes.
Le, grazzi. Ma nixtieqx dan.No, thank you. I do not want this.
Iva, dan tajjeb għalija.Yes, this is good for me.
Grazzi ħafna tal-għajnuna.Thank you very much for the help.
Ta' xejn.You are welcome.
Aħna sejrin nixtru ħwejjeġ ġodda.We are going to buy new clothes.
