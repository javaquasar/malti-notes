Shopping and Clothes

This page helps with buying clothes, asking about colours and sizes, understanding prices, and using safe shopping phrases such as nixtieq and kemm jiswa?.

Shopping Patterns

Nixtieq flokk blu.I would like a blue shirt.
Kemm jiswa dan il-flokk?How much does this shirt cost?
Għandek daqs kbir?Do you have a large size?
Nista' nipprova dan?Can I try this on?
Dialogues

Mini-dialogues

Buying a shirt

A: Nixtieq flokk iswed.A: I would like a black shirt.
B: Liema daqs għandek bżonn?B: What size do you need?
A: Daqs medju, jekk jogħġbok.A: Medium size, please.

Asking the price

A: Kemm jiswa dan iż-żarbun?A: How much does this shoe cost?
B: Jiswa tletin ewro.B: It costs thirty euro.
A: Tajjeb, nieħduh.A: Good, I will take it.
Role Play

Role Play Ideas

Customer

Ask for a colour, a size, and a price. Then decide if you want to buy it.

Shop assistant

Offer another size, another colour, and say the price clearly.
