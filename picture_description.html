Level 1 Picture Description Guide

This page is built for speaking practice. Each scene gives you a simple picture, 20 Maltese sentences, and 20 common questions with English support. The language stays close to Level 1: hemm, qed, family, clothes, colours, places, food, weather, and actions.

5 scene types20 sentences each20 questions eachMaltese + English

How to use it

  • Look at the picture first and speak before reading.
  • Use the sentence bank only when you get stuck.
  • Then answer 5 to 10 questions aloud.
  • Repeat with shorter and faster answers.

Family At Home

Safe Pattern

  • Fl-istampa hemm...
  • Huwa/Hija għandu/għandha...
  • Huwa/Hija qiegħed/qiegħda...
  • Huma qegħdin...

20 Questions

1. Kemm hemm persuni fl-istampa?How many people are there in the picture?
2. Min huma l-persuni fl-istampa?Who are the people in the picture?
3. Fejn qiegħda l-familja?Where is the family?
4. X'qiegħed jagħmel il-missier?What is the father doing?
5. X'qiegħda tagħmel l-omm?What is the mother doing?
6. X'hemm fuq il-mejda?What is there on the table?
7. Hemm ikel fuq il-mejda?Is there food on the table?
8. X'kulur għandu l-qmis tal-missier?What colour is the father's shirt?
9. X'kulur hu l-flokk tal-omm?What colour is the mother's top?
10. X'kulur hu l-flokk tat-tifla?What colour is the girl's top?
11. X'kulur hu l-flokk tat-tifel?What colour is the boy's top?
12. It-tifla qiegħda ħdejn min?Who is the girl next to?
13. It-tifel qiegħed ħdejn min?Who is the boy next to?
14. Hemm tieqa fil-kċina?Is there a window in the kitchen?
15. Hemm friġġ fil-kċina?Is there a fridge in the kitchen?
16. Il-familja tidher ferħana jew imdejqa?Does the family look happy or sad?
17. Il-kċina hija kbira jew żgħira?Is the kitchen big or small?
18. Hemm żewġt itfal jew tliet itfal?Are there two children or three children?
19. Min qiegħed isajjar?Who is cooking?
20. Din hija xena tal-familja?Is this a family scene?

20 Simple Sentences

1. Fl-istampa qed naraw familja fil-kċina.In the picture we can see a family in the kitchen.
2. Hemm omm, missier u żewġt itfal.There is a mother, a father and two children.
3. Il-familja qiegħda d-dar.The family is at home.
4. Il-missier għandu qmis blu.The father has a blue shirt.
5. L-omm għandha flokk aħmar.The mother has a red top.
6. It-tifla għandha flokk roża.The girl has a pink top.
7. It-tifel għandu flokk aħdar.The boy has a green top.
8. Fuq il-mejda hemm ikel.There is food on the table.
9. Fuq il-mejda hemm insalata.There is salad on the table.
10. Hemm ukoll taġen.There is also a pan.
11. Il-missier qiegħed isajjar.The father is cooking.
12. L-omm qiegħda tħares lejn it-tfal.The mother is looking at the children.
13. It-tifla qiegħda ħdejn ommha.The girl is next to her mother.
14. It-tifel qiegħed ħdejn missieru.The boy is next to his father.
15. Il-familja tidher ferħana.The family looks happy.
16. Il-kċina hija kbira u nadifa.The kitchen is big and clean.
17. Hemm tieqa fuq ix-xellug.There is a window on the left.
18. Hemm friġġ fuq il-lemin.There is a fridge on the right.
19. Il-persuni għandhom ħwejjeġ ta' kuluri differenti.The people have clothes of different colours.
20. Din hija xena sabiħa tal-familja.This is a beautiful family scene.

House And Weather

Safe Pattern

  • Fl-istampa hemm...
  • Huwa/Hija għandu/għandha...
  • Huwa/Hija qiegħed/qiegħda...
  • Huma qegħdin...

20 Questions

1. X'hemm fl-istampa?What is in the picture?
2. Hemm dar jew skola?Is there a house or a school?
3. X'kulur għandu s-saqaf?What colour is the roof?
4. X'kulur għandu l-bieb?What colour is the door?
5. Kemm hemm twieqi?How many windows are there?
6. Fejn qiegħda s-siġra?Where is the tree?
7. Fejn qiegħda l-karozza?Where is the car?
8. X'kulur hija l-karozza?What colour is the car?
9. Hemm xemx fis-sema?Is there a sun in the sky?
10. Hemm sħab fis-sema?Are there clouds in the sky?
11. It-temp jidher sabiħ jew ħażin?Does the weather look good or bad?
12. Il-ħaxix huwa aħdar?Is the grass green?
13. Id-dar għandha ġnien?Does the house have a garden?
14. Il-karozza qiegħda quddiem id-dar?Is the car in front of the house?
15. Is-siġra hija kbira jew żgħira?Is the tree big or small?
16. Il-post jidher kwiet?Does the place look quiet?
17. Din hija dar sabiħa?Is this a beautiful house?
18. Id-dar hija bejn xiex u xiex?The house is between what and what?
19. Is-sema hija blu?Is the sky blue?
20. It-temp jidher sħun?Does the weather look warm?

20 Simple Sentences

1. Fl-istampa qed naraw dar.In the picture we can see a house.
2. Id-dar għandha saqaf aħmar.The house has a red roof.
3. Id-dar għandha bieb kannella.The house has a brown door.
4. Hemm żewġ twieqi quddiem id-dar.There are two windows in front of the house.
5. Hemm siġra fuq ix-xellug.There is a tree on the left.
6. Hemm karozza blu fuq il-lemin.There is a blue car on the right.
7. Hemm xemx fis-sema.There is a sun in the sky.
8. Hemm ukoll sħab fis-sema.There are also clouds in the sky.
9. Jidher li t-temp huwa sabiħ.It seems that the weather is nice.
10. Il-ħaxix huwa aħdar.The grass is green.
11. Id-dar għandha ġnien żgħir.The house has a small garden.
12. Il-karozza qiegħda quddiem id-dar.The car is in front of the house.
13. Is-siġra hija kbira.The tree is big.
14. Id-dar tidher sabiħa.The house looks beautiful.
15. Il-post jidher kwiet.The place looks quiet.
16. Din hija dar sabiħa.This is a beautiful house.
17. Id-dar hija bejn is-siġra u l-karozza.The house is between the tree and the car.
18. Is-sema hija blu ċar.The sky is light blue.
19. Il-ġnien huwa nadif u sabiħ.The garden is clean and beautiful.
20. It-temp jidher sħun.The weather looks warm.

20 Simple Sentences

1. Fl-istampa qed naraw erba' persuni fil-park.In the picture we can see four people in the park.
2. Hemm omm, missier, tifla u tifel.There is a mother, a father, a girl and a boy.
3. Huma qegħdin fuq il-ħaxix.They are on the grass.
4. Hemm ballun quddiemhom.There is a ball in front of them.
5. Il-mara għandha libsa ħamra.The woman has a red dress.
6. Ir-raġel għandu flokk blu.The man has a blue top.
7. It-tifla għandha flokk oranġjo.The girl has an orange top.
8. It-tifel għandu flokk aħdar.The boy has a green top.
9. Hemm żewġ siġar fil-park.There are two trees in the park.
10. Hemm xemx fis-sema.There is a sun in the sky.
11. Il-familja tidher ferħana.The family looks happy.
12. It-tfal qegħdin jilagħbu bil-ballun.The children are playing with the ball.
13. Il-ġenituri qegħdin ħdejn it-tfal.The parents are next to the children.
14. Il-park huwa aħdar u sabiħ.The park is green and beautiful.
15. Hemm erba' persuni kollha kemm huma.There are four people altogether.
16. Il-ballun huwa iswed u abjad.The ball is black and white.
17. It-tifel qiegħed fuq il-lemin.The boy is on the right.
18. L-omm qiegħda fuq ix-xellug.The mother is on the left.
19. Din hija xena ta' familja fil-park.This is a family scene in the park.
20. Il-post jidher sabiħ u kwiet.The place looks beautiful and quiet.

At The Clinic

Safe Pattern

  • Fl-istampa hemm...
  • Huwa/Hija għandu/għandha...
  • Huwa/Hija qiegħed/qiegħda...
  • Huma qegħdin...

20 Questions

1. Fejn qegħdin il-persuni?Where are the people?
2. Kemm hemm persuni fil-klinika?How many people are in the clinic?
3. Min huma l-persuni fl-istampa?Who are the people in the picture?
4. X'jagħmel it-tabib?What does the doctor do?
5. X'tagħmel l-infermiera?What does the nurse do?
6. Min qiegħed fin-nofs?Who is in the middle?
7. Min qiegħed fuq ix-xellug?Who is on the left?
8. Min qiegħed fuq il-lemin?Who is on the right?
9. X'kulur għandu l-kowt tat-tabib?What colour is the doctor's coat?
10. X'kulur għandha l-libsa tal-infermiera?What colour is the nurse's uniform?
11. Il-pazjent qiegħed fuq saqqu?Is the patient on a bed?
12. Hemm salib aħmar fuq il-ħajt?Is there a red cross on the wall?
13. It-tabib qiegħed iħares lejn min?Who is the doctor looking at?
14. L-infermiera qiegħda tgħin?Is the nurse helping?
15. Il-pazjent jidher ferħan?Does the patient look happy?
16. Il-klinika tidher nadifa?Does the clinic look clean?
17. Din hija skola jew klinika?Is this a school or a clinic?
18. It-tabib għandu xogħol importanti?Does the doctor have an important job?
19. L-infermiera għandha xogħol importanti wkoll?Does the nurse also have an important job?
20. It-tabib u l-infermiera qegħdin jaħdmu?Are the doctor and the nurse working?

20 Simple Sentences

1. Fl-istampa qed naraw klinika.In the picture we can see a clinic.
2. Hemm tabib, infermiera u pazjent.There is a doctor, a nurse and a patient.
3. It-tabib għandu kowt abjad.The doctor has a white coat.
4. L-infermiera għandha libsa blu ċar.The nurse has a light blue uniform.
5. Il-pazjent qiegħed fuq saqqu. [UNCERTAIN]The patient is on a bed. [UNCERTAIN]
6. Il-pazjent għandu flokk aħdar ċar.The patient has a light green top.
7. It-tabib qiegħed fuq ix-xellug.The doctor is on the left.
8. L-infermiera qiegħda fuq il-lemin.The nurse is on the right.
9. Il-pazjent qiegħed fin-nofs.The patient is in the middle.
10. Hemm salib aħmar fuq il-ħajt.There is a red cross on the wall.
11. It-tabib qiegħed iħares lejn il-pazjent.The doctor is looking at the patient.
12. L-infermiera qiegħda tgħin.The nurse is helping.
13. Il-pazjent ma jidhirx ferħan ħafna.The patient does not look very happy.
14. Il-klinika tidher nadifa.The clinic looks clean.
15. Hemm tliet persuni fl-istampa.There are three people in the picture.
16. It-tabib għandu xogħol importanti.The doctor has an important job.
17. L-infermiera għandha xogħol importanti wkoll.The nurse also has an important job.
18. Din hija xena ta' professjonijiet.This is a scene of professions.
19. Il-post jidher kwiet u serju.The place looks quiet and serious.
20. It-tabib u l-infermiera qegħdin jaħdmu.The doctor and the nurse are working.

20 Questions

1. Fejn qegħdin il-persuni?Where are the people?
2. Kemm hemm persuni fl-istampa?How many people are in the picture?
3. Min qiegħed wara l-mejda?Who is behind the table?
4. Min qiegħda quddiem il-mejda?Who is in front of the table?
5. X'hemm fuq il-mejda?What is on the table?

7. Hemm tuffieħ aħdar?Are there green apples?

12. X'kulur hija l-borża?What colour is the bag?
13. Il-mara qiegħda tixtri?Is the woman shopping?
14. Ir-raġel qiegħed ibigħ il-frott?Is the man selling the fruit?

3. Hemm ħafna frott u ħaxix.There is a lot of fruit and vegetables.
4. Hemm tuffieħ aħdar.There are green apples.
5. Hemm tadam aħmar. [UNCERTAIN]There are red tomatoes. [UNCERTAIN]
6. Hemm frott isfar. [UNCERTAIN]There is yellow fruit. [UNCERTAIN]
7. Ir-raġel għandu flokk blu.The man has a blue top.
8. Il-mara għandha flokk isfar.The woman has a yellow top.
9. Il-mara għandha borża bajda.The woman has a white bag.
10. Il-bejjiegħ qiegħed wara l-mejda.The seller is behind the table.
11. Il-mara qiegħda quddiem il-mejda.The woman is in front of the table.
12. Hemm żewġ persuni fl-istampa.There are two people in the picture.
13. Huma qegħdin jitkellmu.They are talking.
14. Il-post jidher mimli ikel frisk.The place looks full of fresh food.
15. Il-kuluri huma qawwijin.The colours are bright.
16. Is-suq jidher interessanti.The market looks interesting.
17. Il-mejda hija mimlija frott.The table is full of fruit.
18. Il-mara qiegħda tixtri.The woman is shopping.
19. Ir-raġel qiegħed ibigħ il-frott.The man is selling the fruit.
20. Din hija xena ta' ikel u xiri.This is a scene of food and shopping.
Exam Rhythm

Fast Speaking Pattern

1. Fl-istampa hemm...Say what and who is in the picture.
2. Huma qegħdin...Say where they are.
3. Huwa/Hija għandu/għandha...Say clothes, colours, or objects.
4. Huwa/Hija qiegħed/qiegħda...Say the action.
5. Il-post jidher...Finish with a simple opinion: sabiħ, kwiet, interessanti, nadif.
