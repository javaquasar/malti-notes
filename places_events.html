Section 5

Useful Patterns

noun + adjective knisja qadima, mużew interessanti, monument famuż
Jien immur, int tmur, huwa jmur ... Present tense of mar for speaking about where you go.
Inħobb immur + place / event Inħobb immur festivals. / Inħobb immur ġo mużewijiet.
Darba mort + place Darba mort Blue Grotto. / Darba mort Muża.
Meta nkun + place, inħobb + verb Meta nkun ir-Rabat, inħobb inżur il-Katakombi.
F'Malta nistgħu naraw ... F'Malta nistgħu naraw ħafna affarijiet interessanti.
Section 6

Ready-Made Models

Short speaking paragraph

Jien inħobb immur postijiet storiċi.
I like to go to historical places.
L-Imdina hija belt qadima u sabiħa.
Mdina is an old and beautiful city.
Meta nkun ir-Rabat, inħobb inżur il-Katakombi.
When I am in Rabat, I like to visit the Catacombs.
Darba mort Muża nara esebizzjoni tal-arti.
Once I went to Muża to see an art exhibition.
F'Malta hawn ħafna postijiet interessanti.
In Malta there are many interesting places.
