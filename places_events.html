Section 5

Useful Patterns

noun + adjective knisja qadima, mużew interessanti, monument famuż
Jien immur, int tmur, huwa jmur ... Present tense of mar for speaking about where you go.
Inħobb immur + place / event Inħobb immur festivals. / Inħobb immur ġo mużewijiet.
Darba mort + place Darba mort Blue Grotto. / Darba mort Muża.
Meta nkun + place, inħobb + verb Meta nkun ir-Rabat, inħobb inżur il-Katakombi.
F'Malta nistgħu naraw ... F'Malta nistgħu naraw ħafna affarijiet interessanti.
Section 6

Ready-Made Models

Short speaking paragraph

Jien inħobb immur postijiet storiċi.
I like to go to historical places.
L-Imdina hija belt qadima u sabiħa.
Mdina is an old and beautiful city.
Meta nkun ir-Rabat, inħobb inżur il-Katakombi.
When I am in Rabat, I like to visit the Catacombs.
Darba mort Muża nara esebizzjoni tal-arti.
Once I went to Muża to see an art exhibition.
F'Malta hawn ħafna postijiet interessanti.
In Malta there are many interesting places.

Practice Bank

Story Bank

1. F'Malta hawn ħafna postijiet interessanti.In Malta there are many interesting places.
2. L-Imdina hija belt qadima u sabiħa.Mdina is an old and beautiful city.
3. Il-Katakombi huma post storiku famuż.The Catacombs are a famous historical place.
4. Inħobb immur ġo mużewijiet.I like to go to museums.
5. Darba mort Blue Grotto.Once I went to Blue Grotto.
6. Darba mort Muża nara esebizzjoni tal-arti.Once I went to Muża to see an art exhibition.
7. Ġgantija huwa maqdes kbir.Ġgantija is a big temple.
8. L-irdumijiet ta' Ħad-Dingli huma sbieħ ħafna.The Dingli cliffs are very beautiful.
9. Il-veduti huma sbieħ u kwieti.The views are beautiful and quiet.
10. Malta m'għandhiex kaskati kbar.Malta does not have big waterfalls.
11. Inħobb immur festivals fis-sajf.I like to go to festivals in summer.
12. Hemm festival tal-mużika fil-belt.There is a music festival in the city.
13. Xi nies iħobbu festival tal-birra.Some people like the beer festival.
14. Oħrajn iħobbu festival tal-inbid.Others like the wine festival.
15. Il-karnival huwa tradizzjoni popolari.Carnival is a popular tradition.
16. Il-prinjolata hija ħelwa tradizzjonali.Prinjolata is a traditional dessert.
17. Fil-prinjolata hemm krema u ċikkulata.In prinjolata there is cream and chocolate.
18. Xi kultant hemm ukoll ġewż u perlini.Sometimes there are also walnuts and sugar pearls.
19. Jien inħobb postijiet storiċi.I like historical places.
20. Oħti tħobb postijiet moderni.My sister likes modern places.
21. Huwa jmur il-pjazza kull nhar ta' Sibt.He goes to the square every Saturday.
22. Hija tmur il-mużew mal-ħbieb tagħha.She goes to the museum with her friends.
23. Aħna mmorru l-Belt Valletta xi kultant.We go to Valletta sometimes.
24. Intom tmorru l-karnival?Do you go to carnival?
25. Huma jmorru jaraw monument famuż.They go to see a famous monument.
26. F'Malta nistgħu naraw ħafna affarijiet interessanti.In Malta we can see many interesting things.
27. Il-bini modern huwa għoli ħafna.The modern building is very tall.
28. Ir-raħal huwa kwiet u sabiħ.The village is quiet and beautiful.
29. Il-monument huwa famuż.The monument is famous.
30. Il-knisja hija qadima.The church is old.
