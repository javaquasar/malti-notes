Section 1

Core Idea

F'din it-tema nitkellmu fuq l-ikel, ix-xorb, u x'inhuma l-gosti tagħna.
In this topic we talk about food, drink, and what our preferences are.
Nużaw vokabularju komuni tal-ikel, sentenzi dwar il-ħin tal-ġurnata, u djalogi qosra.
We use common food vocabulary, sentences about times of day, and short dialogues.
Il-verbi l-aktar importanti hawn huma niekol, nieħu, u nixrob.
The most important verbs here are niekol, nieħu, and nixrob.

Section 5

Breakfast

Filgħodu nħobb niekol bajda u towst bil-butir.In the morning I like to eat an egg and toast with butter.
Filgħodu nħobb nieħu cereal bil-ħalib.In the morning I like to have cereal with milk.
Filgħodu ma nħobbx niekol.In the morning I do not like to eat.
Filgħodu nieħu kafè u gallettina.In the morning I have coffee and a biscuit.
Section 6

Lunch

F'nofsinhar inħobb niekol xi laħam u ħaxix.At midday I like to eat some meat and vegetables.
F'nofsinhar inħobb nieħu soppa.At midday I like to have soup.
F'nofsinhar nieħu biċċa ħobż bil-butir u ġobon.At midday I have a piece of bread with butter and cheese.
Section 7

Dinner

Filgħaxija ma nħobbx niekol.In the evening I do not like to eat.
Filgħaxija nħobb nieħu xi ħaġa ħafifa, bħal soppa jew brodu.In the evening I like to have something light, such as soup or broth.
Filgħaxija nħobb nieħu xi towst.In the evening I like to have some toast.
Filgħaxija nħobb nieħu tiġieġ.In the evening I like to have chicken.

Section 11

Food Verb Sentences

Ilbieraħ kilt żewġ pastizzi.Yesterday I ate two pastizzi.
Inħobb niekol ikel tradizzjonali Malti.I like to eat traditional Maltese food.
Inħobb nieħu birra filgħaxija.I like to have beer in the evening.
Inħobb nixrob tazza nbid ma' biċċa laħam.I like to drink a glass of wine with a piece of meat.
Mal-ħut inħobb nieħu tazza nbid bajda.With fish I like to have a glass of white wine.
Għandi ikla tax-xogħol u se nieklu pizza.I have a work meal and we are going to eat pizza.
Fil-brejk se niekol ftira biż-żejt.During the break I am going to eat ftira biż-żejt.

L-istuffat tal-fenek

L-istuffat isir billi l-fenek jitqatta' f'biċċiet u jitħalla jissajjar bil-mod f'zalza.
The stew is made by cutting the rabbit into pieces and letting it cook slowly in a sauce.
Iż-zalza ssir mit-tadam, inbid aħmar, tewm, basal u ħwawar.
The sauce is made from tomatoes, red wine, garlic, onions, and spices.
Tradizzjonalment, l-istuffat tal-fenek jiġi servut ma' ħobż frisk Malti.
Traditionally, rabbit stew is served with fresh Maltese bread.

Il-ftira biż-żejt

Il-ftira biż-żejt hija ikla tradizzjonali u sempliċi ħafna fil-kċina ta' Malta.
Ftira biż-żejt is a very traditional and simple meal in Maltese cuisine.
Hija tip ta' ħobż Malti ċatt u tond, b'qoxra kemxejn tqarmeċ.
It is a type of flat and round Maltese bread with a slightly crunchy crust.
Normalment timtela biż-żejt taż-żebbuġa, tadam, tonn, kappar, żebbuġ, basal u xi drabi ġbejniet jew bajd mgħolli.
It is usually filled with olive oil, tomato, tuna, capers, olives, onion, and sometimes cheeselets or boiled eggs.

Section 15

Common Mistakes

Do not mix up niekol, nieħu, and nixrobniekol for eating, nixrob for drinking, and nieħu for taking / having.
Use ma...x in negative food sentencesMa nħobbx niekol, not a direct English-style negative.
Watch the question wordXi tħobb tiekol? is not the same as X'tixtieq? or X'għandek bżonn?.
Watch small food wordsbil-butir, mal-ħut, mat-te, mal-kafè.
