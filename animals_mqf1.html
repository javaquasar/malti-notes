Domestic and Wild Animals

This page is built from Lesson 17 - L-annimali. It keeps the lesson vocabulary for domestic and wild animals, adds a few very common animals already mentioned in the lesson overview, and finishes with 100 simple description sentences for speaking and writing practice.

Practice Bank

50 Domestic Animal Descriptions

Short, course-safe lines for homework and oral practice. The grammar stays simple and close to the lesson.

1. Dan kelb żgħir.This is a small dog.
2. Il-kelb huwa iswed.The dog is black.
3. Il-kelb għandu denb twil.The dog has a long tail.
4. Il-kelb jinbaħ ħafna.The dog barks a lot.
5. Dan il-kelb għandu għajnejn kannella.This dog has brown eyes.
6. Din kelba bajda.This is a white female dog.
7. Il-kelba hija ferħana.The female dog is happy.
8. Għandi kelb id-dar.I have a dog at home.
9. Il-kelb tiegħi jismu Max. [added-example]My dog is called Max. [added-example]
10. Inħobb il-kelb tiegħi għax huwa ħelu.I like my dog because it is cute.
11. Dan qattus kbir.This is a big cat.
12. Din qattusa bajda.This is a white female cat.
13. Il-qattus għandu pil artab. [added-example]The cat has soft fur. [added-example]
14. Il-qattus jingħi filgħodu.The cat meows in the morning.
15. Il-qattusa għandha għajnejn ħodor.The female cat has green eyes.
16. Għandi qattusa d-dar.I have a female cat at home.
17. Il-qattusa tħobb torqod. [added-example]The cat likes to sleep. [added-example]
18. Dan il-qattus għandu denb twil.This cat has a long tail.
19. Il-qtates huma annimali domestiċi popolari.Cats are popular domestic animals.
20. Jien inħobb il-qattus għax huwa kwiet. [added-example]I like the cat because it is quiet. [added-example]
21. Dan għasfur żgħir.This is a small bird.
22. Dawn għasafar bojod.These are white birds.
23. L-għasfur għandu ġwienaħ bojod.The bird has white wings.
24. L-għasfur isaffar.The bird chirps.
25. L-għasfur jgħanni filgħodu.The bird sings in the morning.
26. Dan pappagall aħdar.This is a green parrot.
27. Għandi żewġ pappagalli.I have two parrots.
28. Waħda hija mara u wieħed huwa raġel.One is female and one is male.
29. Il-pappagall jitkellem ftit. [added-example]The parrot speaks a little. [added-example]
30. Il-pappagall għandu munqar oranġjo.The parrot has an orange beak.
31. Dan fenek abjad.This is a white rabbit.
32. Il-fenek għandu widnejn twal. [added-example]The rabbit has long ears. [added-example]
33. Għandi fenek tal-Indi żgħir.I have a small guinea pig.
34. Il-fenek tal-Indi huwa kannella u abjad. [added-example]The guinea pig is brown and white. [added-example]
35. Din ħuta blu.This is a blue fish.
36. Il-ħuta għandha denb aħmar.The fish has a red tail.
37. Il-ħuta tiegħi huwa raġel.My fish is male.
38. Għandi fekruna tal-art.I have a tortoise.
39. Il-fekruna għandha ħames snin.The tortoise is five years old.
40. Il-fekruna għandha ġilda iebsa. [added-example]The tortoise has hard skin. [added-example]
41. Din tiġieġa bajda.This is a white hen.
42. Dan serduq sabiħ.This is a nice rooster.
43. It-tiġieġa għandha rix abjad.The hen has white feathers.
44. Dan żiemel kannella.This is a brown horse.
45. Iż-żiemel huwa kbir u sabiħ.The horse is big and beautiful.
46. Din baqra sewda.This is a black cow.
47. Il-baqra hija annimal kbir.The cow is a big animal.
48. Dan ħanżir roża. [added-example]This is a pink pig. [added-example]
49. L-annimali domestiċi jgħixu mal-bniedem. [added-example]Domestic animals live with people. [added-example]
50. Jien inħobb l-annimali domestiċi għax huma ħelwin u sbieħ. [added-example]I like domestic animals because they are cute and beautiful. [added-example]

50 Wild or Zoo Animal Descriptions

Use these lines for zoo pictures, wild animal descriptions, and short speaking answers.

1. Dan iljun kbir.This is a big lion.
2. L-iljun għandu denb twil.The lion has a long tail.
3. L-iljun huwa annimal slavaġ.The lion is a wild animal.
4. L-iljuni jgħixu fiż-żoo jew fis-selvaġġ. [added-example]Lions live in the zoo or in the wild. [added-example]
5. Din tigra oranġjo u sewda.This is an orange and black tiger.
6. It-tigra għandha ġilda sabiħa. [added-example]The tiger has beautiful skin. [added-example]
7. It-tigra għandha denb twil.The tiger has a long tail.
8. It-tigri huma annimali slavaġ.Tigers are wild animals.
9. Din ġiraffa twila.This is a tall giraffe.
10. Il-ġiraffa għandha għonq twil ħafna.The giraffe has a very long neck.
11. Il-ġiraffa għandha ġilda kannella u bajda. [added-example]The giraffe has brown and white skin. [added-example]
12. Dawn ġiraffi kbar.These are big giraffes.
13. Din żebra sabiħa.This is a beautiful zebra.
14. Iż-żebra għandha kulur iswed u abjad.The zebra has black and white colour.
15. Iż-żebra għandha erba’ saqajn.The zebra has four legs.
16. Dawn żebri fiż-żoo.These are zebras in the zoo.
17. Dan iljunfant kbir ħafna.This is a very big elephant.
18. L-iljunfant għandu widnejn kbar. [added-example]The elephant has big ears. [added-example]
19. L-iljunfant għandu denb qasir. [added-example]The elephant has a short tail. [added-example]
20. L-iljunfanti huma annimali kbar.Elephants are big animals.
21. Dan kukkudrill aħdar.This is a green crocodile.
22. Il-kukkudrill għandu denb twil.The crocodile has a long tail.
23. Il-kukkudrill jgħix fl-ilma. [added-example]The crocodile lives in water. [added-example]
24. Dawn kukkudrilli kbar.These are big crocodiles.
25. Din ajkla kbira.This is a big eagle.
26. L-ajkla għandha ġwienaħ kbar.The eagle has big wings.
27. L-ajkla għandha munqar qawwi. [added-example]The eagle has a strong beak. [added-example]
28. L-ajkli huma għasafar slavaġ.Eagles are wild birds.
29. Din balena kbira ħafna.This is a very big whale.
30. Il-balena tgħix fil-baħar. [added-example]The whale lives in the sea. [added-example]
31. Il-balena għandha denb kbir.The whale has a big tail.
32. Dan pingwin żgħir.This is a small penguin.
33. Il-pingwin huwa iswed u abjad.The penguin is black and white.
34. Il-pingwin jgħix f’post kiesaħ. [added-example]The penguin lives in a cold place. [added-example]
35. Dawn pingwini fil-grupp.These are penguins in a group.
36. Dan pagun sabiħ ħafna.This is a very beautiful peacock.
37. Il-pagun għandu rix sabiħ.The peacock has beautiful feathers.
38. Il-pagun għandu kuluri qawwijin. [added-example]The peacock has bright colours. [added-example]
39. Din xadina ħelwa.This is a cute monkey.
40. Ix-xadina għandha denb twil.The monkey has a long tail.
41. Ix-xadina tħobb titla’. [added-example]The monkey likes to climb. [added-example]
42. Dawn xadini fil-gaġġa. [added-example]These are monkeys in the cage. [added-example]
43. Din gurilla kbira.This is a big gorilla.
44. Il-gurilla għandha dirgħajn twal. [added-example]The gorilla has long arms. [added-example]
45. Il-gurilla hija annimal qawwi. [added-example]The gorilla is a strong animal. [added-example]
46. Dan ippopotamu kbir. [overview-based]This is a big hippopotamus. [overview-based]
47. Ir-rinoċeront għandu ġilda griża. [overview-based]The rhinoceros has grey skin. [overview-based]
48. L-annimali slavaġ ma jgħixux id-dar. [added-example]Wild animals do not live at home. [added-example]
49. Ħafna annimali slavaġ jinsabu fiż-żoo.Many wild animals are in the zoo.
50. Jien inħobb immur fiż-żoo biex nara l-annimali. [added-example]I like to go to the zoo to see the animals. [added-example]
