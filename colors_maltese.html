Shades and Useful Words

Shade Words

  • skur = dark
  • ċar = light / pale
  • qawwi = strong / bright
  • ikkulurit = colourful

Useful Combinations

  • blu skur = dark blue
  • aħdar ċar = light green
  • aħmar qawwi = bright red
  • kamra ikkulurita = colourful room
Patterns

Useful Colour Patterns

X'kulur hu? What colour is it?
Huwa blu. It is blue.
Din il-karozza ħamra. This car is red.
Inħobb il-kulur aħdar. I like the colour green.
Nixtieq qmis abjad. I would like a white shirt.
Il-bieb kannella skur. The door is dark brown.

Speaking

Questions and Answers

X'kulur hu l-qmis? Il-qmis abjad. = The shirt is white.
X'kulur hi l-karozza? Il-karozza sewda. = The car is black.
Liema kulur tħobb? Jien inħobb il-blu. = I like blue.
Tixtieq qmis aħmar jew blu? Nixtieq qmis blu. = I would like a blue shirt.
Wrong vs Right

Common Mistakes

Wrong: Il-karozza huwa aħmar. Right: Il-karozza ħamra.
Wrong: Jien nixtieq blu qmis. Right: Nixtieq qmis blu.
Wrong: Il-bieb blu skura. Right: Il-bieb blu skur.
Wrong: Din aħdar borża. Right: Din borża ħadra.
