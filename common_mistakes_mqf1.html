The goal is not perfect theory. The goal is to catch the mistakes that appear again and again in speaking and homework.

Mistake 1

qiegħed / qiegħda / qegħdin

hu qiegħed | hi qiegħda | huma qegħdin

Common Problem

Hi qiegħed tixtri.
Wrong: feminine subject with masculine form.
Hi qiegħda tixtri.
Right: feminine subject takes qiegħda.

More Examples

  • Hu qiegħed jiekol.
  • Hi qiegħda tixrob.
  • Huma qegħdin jimxu.
Mistake 2

għandi vs hemm

għandi = I have

  • Għandi karozza.
  • Għandi oħt.
  • Għandi uġigħ f'rasi.

hemm = there is / there are

  • Hemm karozza quddiem id-dar.
  • Hemm oħt fl-istampa.
  • Hemm ħafna nies hawn.
Wrong: Hemm karozza tiegħi. | Better: Għandi karozza.
Mistake 3

se vs Present

Present

  • Illum niekol id-dar.
  • Filgħodu naħdem.
  • Issa nixrob te.

Future

  • Għada se niekol id-dar.
  • Għada se naħdem.
  • Aktar tard se nixrob te.
Mistake 4

Time Forms

Illum se mort il-belt.
Wrong: mixed future marker with past form.
Illum mort il-belt.
Past only.
Għada se mmur il-belt.
Future only.
  • ilbieraħ -> yesterday
  • illum -> today
  • għada -> tomorrow
  • issa -> now
Mistake 5

Gender Agreement

People

Il-mara huwa ferħan.
Wrong subject and adjective agreement.
Il-mara hija ferħana.
Right feminine form.

Picture Description

It-tifla qiegħed tgħin.
Wrong continuous form.
It-tifla qiegħda tgħin.
Right feminine form.
Mistake 6

Articles and Prepositions

Use the Combined Form

  • fil-kċina not fi il-kċina
  • mal-familja not ma il-familja
  • mill-belt not minn il-belt

Useful Checks

  • after fi, often check fil- or f'
  • after ma', often check mal-
  • after minn, often check mill-, mit-, mis-
Final Check

Quick Check Before You Finish

  • Did I choose the right subject: hu, hi, or huma?
  • Do I need qiegħed, qiegħda, or qegħdin?
  • Am I talking about now, the past, or the future?
  • Do I mean I have or there is?
  • Did I use the combined article after the preposition?
