Directions and Places in Town

This page helps you ask the way, give directions, and talk about common places in town. It is especially useful for speaking practice, transport questions, and simple real-life situations.

left / right straight on near / far asking the way

Useful speaking idea

  • Start with Skużani or Fejn hu....
  • Use one direction at a time.
  • Keep the route simple and safe.

Asking

Asking the Way

Skużani, fejn hi l-ispiżerija?Excuse me, where is the pharmacy?
Fejn hu r-ristorant?Where is the restaurant?
Il-bank huwa qrib?Is the bank near?
Giving

Giving Directions

Mur dritt.Go straight on.
Imbagħad iddur fuq ix-xellug.Then turn left.
Wara, iddur fuq il-lemin.After that, turn right.
Il-bank huwa faċċata tal-knisja.The bank is opposite the church.
Dialogues

Mini-dialogues

Bus stop

A: Skużani, fejn hi l-waqfa tal-linja?A: Excuse me, where is the bus stop?
B: Mur dritt u iddur fuq il-lemin.B: Go straight on and turn right.
B: Hija ħdejn il-bank.B: It is next to the bank.

Pharmacy

A: Fejn hi l-ispiżerija?A: Where is the pharmacy?
B: Hija qrib ħafna.B: It is very near.
B: Hija faċċata tal-iskola.B: It is opposite the school.
Routes

Route Examples

Mill-waqfa tal-linja mur dritt sal-kantuniera.From the bus stop go straight on until the corner.
Fuq ix-xellug tara l-bank.On the left you will see the bank.
Ir-ristorant huwa wara l-bank.The restaurant is behind the bank.
Review

Wrong vs Right

Wrong

Mur fuq ix-xellug dritt.Mixed order and unclear direction.

Right

Mur dritt u mbagħad iddur fuq ix-xellug.Go straight on and then turn left.
Phrases

50 Thematic Phrases

Skużani, fejn hu l-bank?Excuse me, where is the bank?
Fejn hi l-ispiżerija?Where is the pharmacy?
Fejn hi l-waqfa tal-linja?Where is the bus stop?
Fejn hu r-ristorant?Where is the restaurant?
Fejn hi l-iskola?Where is the school?
Mur dritt.Go straight on.
Ibqa' sejjer dritt.Keep going straight on.
Iddur fuq ix-xellug.Turn left.
Iddur fuq il-lemin.Turn right.
Imbagħad mur dritt mill-ġdid.Then go straight again.
Il-bank huwa qrib ħafna.The bank is very near.
L-ispiżerija mhijiex 'il bogħod.The pharmacy is not far away.
Ir-ristorant huwa faċċata tal-knisja.The restaurant is opposite the church.
Il-waqfa tal-linja hija ħdejn il-bank.The bus stop is next to the bank.
L-iskola hija wara l-ġnien.The school is behind the garden.
Il-bank huwa fuq ix-xellug.The bank is on the left.
Il-kafetterija hija fuq il-lemin.The café is on the right.
Il-knisja hija quddiemek.The church is in front of you.
Il-mużew huwa qrib il-baħar.The museum is near the sea.
Il-ħanut huwa fil-pjazza.The shop is in the square.
Mur sal-kantuniera.Go until the corner.
Wara l-kantuniera iddur fuq il-lemin.After the corner turn right.
Wara l-bank tara l-waqfa tal-linja.After the bank you will see the bus stop.
Quddiem l-iskola hemm triq żgħira.In front of the school there is a small street.
Il-lukanda hija qrib l-ajruport.The hotel is near the airport.
L-istazzjon huwa 'il bogħod.The station is far away.
Il-ħabib tiegħi jgħix qrib il-knisja.My friend lives near the church.
Jien se mmur il-bank illum.I will go to the bank today.
Aħna sejrin ir-ristorant bil-mixi.We are going to the restaurant on foot.
Huwa qiegħed ifittex l-ispiżerija.He is looking for the pharmacy.
Hi trid tmur il-bank.She wants to go to the bank.
Nista' mmur hemm bil-karozza.I can go there by car.
Nista' mmur hemm bil-linja wkoll.I can also go there by bus.
Din it-triq twassal sal-pjazza.This road leads to the square.
It-triq l-oħra twassal sal-baħar.The other road leads to the sea.
Ara s-sinjali fit-triq.Look at the signs on the road.
Staqsi lil xi ħadd fit-triq.Ask someone in the street.
Il-post huwa faċli biex issibu.The place is easy to find.
Il-post mhuwiex faċli biex issibu.The place is not easy to find.
Mill-pjazza mur dritt sal-bank.From the square go straight to the bank.
Mill-bank iddur fuq ix-xellug lejn il-knisja.From the bank turn left toward the church.
Mill-knisja mur dritt lejn il-waqfa tal-linja.From the church go straight toward the bus stop.
Il-librerija hija bejn il-bank u l-iskola.The library is between the bank and the school.
Il-kafetterija hija wara l-librerija.The café is behind the library.
Il-mużew huwa quddiem il-pjazza.The museum is in front of the square.
Il-bus stop huwa ħdejn il-ħanut.The bus stop is next to the shop.
L-ispiżerija tiftaħ kmieni filgħodu.The pharmacy opens early in the morning.
Ir-ristorant jagħlaq tard filgħaxija.The restaurant closes late in the evening.
Jekk jogħġbok, tista' tgħinni?Please, can you help me?
Żgur, ngħinek.Of course, I will help you.
Grazzi ħafna tal-għajnuna.Thank you very much for the help.
Ta' xejn.You are welcome.
