Dialogues

Mini-dialogues

Late bus

A: Għaliex wasalt tard?A: Why did you arrive late?
B: Għax il-linja waslet tard.B: Because the bus arrived late.
A: Nifhem. Illum hemm traffiku ħafna.A: I understand. Today there is a lot of traffic.

Lost keys

A: X'hemm?A: What is wrong?
B: Tlift iċ-ċwievet.B: I lost the keys.
A: Tista' tfittex fil-borża?A: Can you look in the bag?
Review

Wrong vs Right

Wrong

Jien tlift il-keys.Mixed language.

Right

Tlift iċ-ċwievet.I lost the keys.
