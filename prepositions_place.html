Prepositions of Place

This page helps you describe where things are: on the table, under the chair, next to the window, behind the sofa, in front of the house, and between two objects. These are some of the most useful building blocks for picture description and home vocabulary.

fuq taħt ħdejn quddiem wara bejn

Best way to use this page

  • Say one short sentence for each preposition.
  • Use furniture words you already know.
  • Start with hemm and then expand.
  • Use this page together with Home and Picture Description.

Il-lampa hija ħdejn is-sodda.The lamp is next to the bed.
Il-karozza hija quddiem id-dar.The car is in front of the house.
Il-ġnien huwa wara d-dar.The garden is behind the house.
Is-siġġu huwa bejn il-mejda u t-tieqa.The chair is between the table and the window.

Rooms

Room Position

Il-kċina hija ħdejn is-salott.The kitchen is next to the living room.
Il-kamra tal-banju hija wara l-kċina.The bathroom is behind the kitchen.
Il-kamra tas-sodda hija quddiem il-kuritur.The bedroom is in front of the corridor.
Il-kamra tal-ikel hija bejn il-kċina u s-salott.The dining room is between the kitchen and the living room.
Speaking

Picture Description Models

Fl-istampa hemm mejda u żewġ siġġijiet. Is-siġġijiet huma ħdejn il-mejda.In the picture there is a table and two chairs. The chairs are next to the table.
Hemm tieqa kbira wara s-sufan.There is a big window behind the sofa.
Fuq il-mejda hemm ktieb u tazza.On the table there is a book and a cup.
Taħt il-mejda hemm borża żgħira.Under the table there is a small bag.
Review

Wrong vs Right

Wrong

Il-ktieb fuq il-mejda huwa.Wrong word order.
Il-lampa hija taħt is-sodda fuq.Do not stack prepositions randomly.

Right

Il-ktieb huwa fuq il-mejda.The book is on the table.
Il-lampa hija ħdejn is-sodda.The lamp is next to the bed.
