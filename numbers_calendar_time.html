Seasons

Seasons of the Year

Vocabulary

ir-rebbiegħaspring
is-sajfsummer
il-ħarifaautumn
ix-xitwawinter

Simple Examples

  • Fis-sajf inħobb immur il-baħar. = In summer I like to go to the sea.
  • Fix-xitwa noqgħod id-dar. = In winter I stay at home.
  • Ir-rebbiegħa hija sabiħa. = Spring is beautiful.
  • Il-ħarifa hija kwieta. = Autumn is quiet.

Clock Time

Telling the Time in Maltese

Full Hours

1:00is-siegħa
2:00is-sagħtejn
3:00it-tlieta
4:00l-erbgħa
5:00il-ħamsa
6:00is-sitta
7:00is-sebgħa
8:00it-tmienja
9:00id-disgħa
10:00l-għaxra
11:00il-ħdax
12:00pmnofsinhar
12:00amnofsillejl

Parts of the Day

ta' filgħoduin the morning
ta' waranofsinharin the afternoon
ta' filgħaxijain the evening
ta' billejlat night
  • is-sitta ta' filgħodu = six in the morning
  • l-erbgħa ta' waranofsinhar = four in the afternoon
  • id-disgħa ta' billejl = nine at night

u = past

[hour] + u + [minutes]
  • it-tlieta u ħamsa = five past three
  • l-erbgħa u għaxra = ten past four
  • il-ħamsa u kwart = quarter past five
  • it-tmienja u nofs = half past eight

neqsin = to

[next hour] + neqsin + [minutes]
  • il-ħamsa neqsin ħamsa = five to five
  • is-sebgħa neqsin kwart = quarter to seven
  • it-tmienja neqsin għoxrin = twenty to eight
  • l-erbgħa neqsin għaxra = ten to four

Practice

Ready-Made Models

Date and Calendar

  • Illum it-Tlieta. = Today is Tuesday.
  • Għandi appuntament f'April. = I have an appointment in April.
  • Fis-sajf inħobb immur il-baħar. = In summer I like to go to the sea.
  • Il-ġimgħa d-dieħla se mmur Għawdex. = Next week I will go to Gozo.

Time and Routine

  • X'ħin hu? = What time is it’
  • Huwa l-erbgħa u għaxra. = It is ten past four.
  • Filgħodu nqum fis-sebgħa. = In the morning I wake up at seven.
  • Ilbieraħ mort il-ħanut fl-għaxra ta' billejl. = Yesterday I went to the shop at ten at night.
