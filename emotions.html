Section 1

What Emotions Are

Bħala umani nħossu emozzjonijiet differenti.
As humans, we feel different emotions.
L-emozzjonijiet jiġu meta nkunu ferħanin jew imdejqin.
Emotions come when we are happy or sad.
Meta nkunu fil-post favorit tagħna, pereżempju, inħossuna ferħanin.
When we are in our favourite place, for example, we feel happy.
Jekk inkunu ħdejn il-baħar, nirrilassaw u nħossuna kalmi.
If we are near the sea, we relax and feel calm.

I feel happy. (male speaker)

Section 6

Situation Models

Meta nkun fil-post favorit tiegħi, inħossni ferħan / ferħana.When I am in my favourite place, I feel happy.
Meta nkun ħdejn il-baħar, inħossni kalm / kalma.When I am near the sea, I feel calm.
Meta jkolli ħafna xogħol, inħossni inkwetat / inkwetata.When I have a lot of work, I feel worried.
Meta niltaqa' mal-ħbieb, inħossni kuntent / kuntenta.When I meet my friends, I feel content.
Meta jkolli aħbar tajba, inħossni eċitat / eċitata.When I have good news, I feel excited.
Meta nagħmel żball, inħossni imbarazzat / imbarazzata.When I make a mistake, I feel embarrassed.

Section 9

Individual Speaking

Illum inħossni ferħan / ferħana għax il-ġurnata tiegħi sejra tajjeb.Today I feel happy because my day is going well.
Xi kultant inħossni nervuż / nervuża, imma meta nitkellem mal-ħbieb nikkalma.Sometimes I feel nervous, but when I speak with friends I calm down.
Meta nkun ħdejn il-baħar, inħossni rilassat / rilassata u kalm / kalma.When I am near the sea, I feel relaxed and calm.
Ma nħobbx inħossni imbarazzat / imbarazzata, imma kultant jiġri.I do not like feeling embarrassed, but sometimes it happens.
Section 10

Common Mistakes

Do not forget gender agreement Jien inħossni ferħan for a male speaker, but Jien inħossni ferħana for a female speaker.
Do not mix up the pronoun endings tħossok, tħossha, and jħossu are different forms.
Use inħossni when you mean “I feel” Jien ferħan and Jien inħossni ferħan are close, but the lesson focuses on the second pattern.
Watch spelling in difficult emotions Words like nervuż, mistħi, imdejjaq, and imbarazzat need careful spelling.
