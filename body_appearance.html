Male speaker / male person

Female speaker / female person

Q&A

Questions and Answers

Questions

Kif jidher?What does he look like?
Kif tidher?What does she look like?
Għandu xagħar twil jew qasir?Does he have long or short hair?
Għandha gċajnejn kbar?Does she have big eyes?

Answers

Huwa twil u għandu xagħar qasir.He is tall and he has short hair.
Hija qasira u għandha gċajnejn kbar.She is short and she has big eyes.
Iva, għandha xagħar twil.Yes, she has long hair.
Le, għandu xagħar qasir.No, he has short hair.
