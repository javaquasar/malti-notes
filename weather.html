Fix-xitwa it-temp ikun kiesaħ.In winter the weather is cold.
Fir-rebbiegħa t-temp ikun sabiħ.In spring the weather is nice.
Fil-ħarifa tagħmel aktar xita.In autumn it rains more.
Daily Plans

Weather and Daily Plans

Jekk it-temp ikun sabiħ, immur il-baħar.If the weather is nice, I go to the sea.
Jekk tagħmel xita, nibqa' d-dar.If it rains, I stay at home.
Jekk ikun kiesaħ, nilbes ġakketta.If it is cold, I wear a jacket.
Jekk ikun sħun, nixrob ħafna ilma.If it is hot, I drink a lot of water.
Dialogues

Mini-dialogues

Morning small talk

A: Kif inhu t-temp illum?A: What is the weather like today?
B: Illum sabiħ u xemxi.B: Today it is nice and sunny.
A: Mela noħroġ wara x-xogħol.A: Then I will go out after work.

Rainy day

A: Qed tagħmel xita?A: Is it raining?
B: Iva, u hawn riħ ukoll.B: Yes, and it is windy too.
A: Mela nibqa' d-dar illum.A: Then I will stay at home today.
