Home, Rooms and Furniture

This page is designed for describing a home in simple Maltese: the outside parts of the house, the rooms inside, and the most useful furniture words. It is especially helpful for picture description, homework, and short speaking answers about where you live.

Best way to use this page

  • Learn 5 words and then say one sentence with each word.
  • Reuse hemm, għandi, fil-, and ħdejn.
  • Start with short safe descriptions before you build longer answers.

Questions

Questions And Answers For Small Talk And Homework

Questions

Fejn toqgħod?Where do you live?
Toqgħod f'dar jew f'appartament?Do you live in a house or in an apartment?
Id-dar tiegħek hija kbira jew żgħira?Is your house big or small?
Kemm hemm kmamar fid-dar tiegħek?How many rooms are there in your home?
Xi hemm fil-kċina?What is in the kitchen?
Xi hemm fis-salott?What is in the living room?

Answers

Noqgħod f'appartament f'Tas-Sliema.I live in an apartment in Sliema.
Id-dar tiegħi hija żgħira imma komda.My home is small but comfortable.
Għandi kċina, salott, u żewġ kmamar tas-sodda.I have a kitchen, a living room, and two bedrooms.
Fil-kċina hemm mejda, siġġijiet, u friġġ.In the kitchen there is a table, chairs, and a fridge.
Fis-salott hemm sufan u televixin.In the living room there is a sofa and a television.
Il-kamra favorita tiegħi hija l-kamra tas-sodda.My favourite room is the bedroom.
Examples

34 Outside And Exterior Examples

Jien noqgħod f'dar kbira.I live in a big house.
Hi toqgħod f'appartament żgħir.She lives in a small apartment.
Id-dar tiegħi hija sabiħa.My house is beautiful.
Id-dar tagħna hija qrib il-baħar.Our house is near the sea.
Quddiem id-dar hemm bieb kbir.In front of the house there is a big door.
Il-bieb huwa kannella skur.The door is dark brown.
Fuq il-bieb hemm numru żgħir.Above the door there is a small number.
Ħdejn il-bieb hemm tieqa.Next to the door there is a window.
It-tieqa hija miftuħa.The window is open.
It-tieqa għandha purtieri bojod.The window has white curtains.
Is-saqaf tad-dar huwa aħmar.The roof of the house is red.
Is-saqaf huwa għoli.The roof is high.
Id-dar għandha gallarija żgħira.The house has a small balcony.
Fuq il-gallarija hemm siġġu wieħed.On the balcony there is one chair.
Xi kultant noqgħod fuq il-gallarija filgħodu.Sometimes I stay on the balcony in the morning.
Wara d-dar hemm ġnien.Behind the house there is a garden.
Il-ġnien huwa nadif u sabiħ.The garden is clean and beautiful.
Fil-ġnien hemm siġar u fjuri.In the garden there are trees and flowers.
Fil-ġnien hemm mejda żgħira.In the garden there is a small table.
Nixrob kafè fil-ġnien meta t-temp ikun sabiħ.I drink coffee in the garden when the weather is nice.
Fuq barra d-dar hemm dawl isfar.Outside the house there is a yellow light.
Il-ħajt ta' barra huwa abjad.The outside wall is white.
Il-garaxx huwa taħt id-dar.The garage is under the house.
Fil-garaxx hemm karozza waħda.There is one car in the garage.
Il-bieb tal-garaxx huwa griż.The garage door is grey.
Quddiem il-garaxx hemm triq kwieta.In front of the garage there is a quiet street.
Id-dar ta' nannuwi għandha ġnien kbir.My grandparents' house has a big garden.
Id-dar tiegħi m'għandhiex garaxx.My house does not have a garage.
L-appartament tagħna għandu gallarija sabiħa.Our apartment has a beautiful balcony.
Mit-tieqa nara t-triq.From the window I can see the street.
Mit-tieqa nara l-baħar.From the window I can see the sea.
Inħobb id-dar tiegħi għax hija komda.I like my home because it is comfortable.
Id-dar hija żgħira, imma hija sabiħa ħafna.The house is small, but it is very beautiful.
Filgħaxija d-dawl ta' barra jkun mixgħul.In the evening the outside light is on.
Examples

33 Room Description Examples

Fil-kċina hemm mejda kbira.In the kitchen there is a big table.
Fil-kċina hemm friġġ abjad.In the kitchen there is a white fridge.
Fil-kċina hemm erba' siġġijiet.There are four chairs in the kitchen.
Il-kċina hija nadifa u sabiħa.The kitchen is clean and beautiful.
Aħna nieklu fil-kamra tal-ikel.We eat in the dining room.
Fil-kamra tal-ikel hemm mejda twila.In the dining room there is a long table.
Il-kamra tal-ikel hija qrib il-kċina.The dining room is near the kitchen.
Fis-salott hemm sufan komdu.In the living room there is a comfortable sofa.
Fis-salott hemm televixin kbir.In the living room there is a big television.
Aħna naraw it-televixin fis-salott.We watch television in the living room.
Fis-salott hemm tieqa kbira.In the living room there is a big window.
Il-kamra tas-sodda tiegħi hija żgħira.My bedroom is small.
Fil-kamra tas-sodda hemm sodda waħda.There is one bed in the bedroom.
Fil-kamra tas-sodda hemm armarju kbir.There is a big wardrobe in the bedroom.
Fuq il-komodina hemm lampa.There is a lamp on the bedside table.
Il-kamra tas-sodda hija kwieta ħafna.The bedroom is very quiet.
Norqod fil-kamra tas-sodda kull lejl.I sleep in the bedroom every night.
Il-kamra tal-banju hija żgħira imma nadifa.The bathroom is small but clean.
Fil-kamra tal-banju hemm mera.There is a mirror in the bathroom.
Fil-kamra tal-banju hemm sink u doċċa.In the bathroom there is a sink and a shower.
Naħsel snieni fil-kamra tal-banju.I brush my teeth in the bathroom.
L-istudju tiegħi għandu skrivanija żgħira.My study has a small desk.
Fl-istudju hemm laptop u kotba.In the study there is a laptop and books.
Naħdem fl-istudju filgħodu.I work in the study in the morning.
Naqra ktieb fl-istudju filgħaxija.I read a book in the study in the evening.
Il-kuritur huwa twil u dejjaq.The corridor is long and narrow.
Mill-kuritur tista' tmur għall-kċina.From the corridor you can go to the kitchen.
Mill-kuritur tista' tmur għas-salott ukoll.From the corridor you can also go to the living room.
Il-kċina hija l-kamra favorita ta' ommi.The kitchen is my mother's favourite room.
Is-salott huwa l-akbar kamra fid-dar.The living room is the biggest room in the house.
Il-kamra tas-sodda tiegħi għandha tieqa kbira.My bedroom has a big window.
Fil-kamra tal-ikel nieklu flimkien.We eat together in the dining room.
Kull kamra fid-dar għandha dawl tajjeb.Every room in the house has good light.
Examples

33 Furniture And Object Examples

Il-mejda hija fil-kċina.The table is in the kitchen.
Il-mejda hija kannella.The table is brown.
Hemm ktieb fuq il-mejda.There is a book on the table.
Hemm tazza fuq il-mejda.There is a cup on the table.
Is-siġġu huwa ħdejn il-mejda.The chair is next to the table.
Għandi sitt siġġijiet fil-kamra tal-ikel.I have six chairs in the dining room.
Is-sufan huwa komdu ħafna.The sofa is very comfortable.
Il-qattus jorqod fuq is-sufan.The cat sleeps on the sofa.
Quddiem is-sufan hemm mejda żgħira.In front of the sofa there is a small table.
Is-sodda tiegħi hija kbira.My bed is big.
Il-kutra hija fuq is-sodda.The blanket is on the bed.
Żewġ imħaded huma fuq is-sodda.Two pillows are on the bed.
L-armarju huwa ħdejn is-sodda.The wardrobe is next to the bed.
Fl-armarju għandi ħafna ħwejjeġ.I have many clothes in the wardrobe.
Il-bieb tal-armarju huwa magħluq.The wardrobe door is closed.
Il-lampa hija fuq il-komodina.The lamp is on the bedside table.
Il-lampa hija safra.The lamp is yellow.
Filgħaxija nixgħel il-lampa.In the evening I switch on the lamp.
L-iskrivanija hija quddiem it-tieqa.The desk is in front of the window.
Fuq l-iskrivanija hemm laptop.There is a laptop on the desk.
Naħdem fuq l-iskrivanija tiegħi kuljum.I work at my desk every day.
Il-friġġ huwa fil-kċina.The fridge is in the kitchen.
Fil-friġġ hemm ħalib u ġobon.There is milk and cheese in the fridge.
Il-friġġ huwa ġdid.The fridge is new.
Ix-xkaffa hija fuq il-ħajt.The shelf is on the wall.
Fuq ix-xkaffa hemm kotba.There are books on the shelf.
Fuq ix-xkaffa hemm pjanta żgħira.There is a small plant on the shelf.
Il-mera hija fil-kamra tal-banju.The mirror is in the bathroom.
Il-mera hija kbira u nadifa.The mirror is big and clean.
Nara wiċċi fil-mera.I see my face in the mirror.
Il-purtieri huma ħdejn it-tieqa.The curtains are next to the window.
It-televixin huwa fis-salott.The television is in the living room.
Il-bank żgħir huwa fil-ġnien.The small bench is in the garden.
