Questions

Questions And Answers For Small Talk And Homework

Questions

Fejn toqgħod?Where do you live?
Toqgħod f'dar jew f'appartament?Do you live in a house or in an apartment?
Id-dar tiegħek hija kbira jew żgħira?Is your house big or small?
Kemm hemm kmamar fid-dar tiegħek?How many rooms are there in your home?
Xi hemm fil-kċina?What is in the kitchen?
Xi hemm fis-salott?What is in the living room?

Answers

Noqgħod f'appartament f'Tas-Sliema.I live in an apartment in Sliema.
Id-dar tiegħi hija żgħira imma komda.My home is small but comfortable.
Għandi kċina, salott, u żewġ kmamar tas-sodda.I have a kitchen, a living room, and two bedrooms.
Fil-kċina hemm mejda, siġġijiet, u friġġ.In the kitchen there is a table, chairs, and a fridge.
Fis-salott hemm sufan u televixin.In the living room there is a sofa and a television.
Il-kamra favorita tiegħi hija l-kamra tas-sodda.My favourite room is the bedroom.
