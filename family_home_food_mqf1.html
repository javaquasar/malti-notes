Likes And Dislikes

Ma nħobbx + noun / verbMa nħobbx il-kafè. / Ma nħobbx niekol ħafna laħam.
Jien inħobb...Use this for simple personal answers.
Jien ma nħobbx...Use this for simple negative answers.
Inħobb il-pizza.I like pizza.
Ma nħobbx it-te.I do not like tea.
Inħobb niekol mal-familja.I like eating with the family.
Ma nħobbx niekol tard. [UNCERTAIN]I do not like eating late. [UNCERTAIN]
Homework

Ready-Made Homework Answers

Il-familja tiegħi hija żgħira. Għandi omm, missier u ħu. Aħna noqogħdu f'dar kbira.My family is small. I have a mother, a father and a brother. We live in a big house.
Id-dar tiegħi għandha kċina, salott u kamra tas-sodda. Hemm mejda fil-kċina u saqqu fil-kamra tas-sodda.My house has a kitchen, a living room and a bedroom. There is a table in the kitchen and a bed in the bedroom.
Inħobb il-frott, il-ħobż u l-ġobon. Filgħodu nieħu kafè u niekol bajda.I like fruit, bread and cheese. In the morning I have coffee and eat an egg.
Ma nħobbx ħafna te, imma nħobb ilma u meraq.I do not like tea very much, but I like water and juice.
Jien inħobb niekol mal-familja tiegħi d-dar.I like eating with my family at home.

Jien għandi żewġt itfal u qattus. I have two children and a cat.
It-tfal jilagħbu fis-salott. The children play in the living room.
Il-qattus joqgħod taħt il-mejda. The cat stays under the table.
Ommi tiġi għandna darbtejn fil-ġimgħa. My mother comes to us twice a week.
ħija tgħin mat-tfal. She helps with the children.
Jien naħdem filgħodu. I work in the morning.
żewġi jaħdem wara nofsinhar. My husband works in the afternoon.
Filgħaxija kulħadd ikun id-dar. In the evening everyone is at home.
Aħna nieklu u nitkellmu flimkien. We eat and talk together.
Id-dar tagħna dejjem tkun mimlija ħajja. Our home is always full of life.
Story 24
Aħna familja mingħajr tfal. We are a family without children.
Aħna nħobbu l-kwiet u l-ordni. We like peace and order.
Id-dar tagħna hija żgħira izda komda. Our home is small but comfortable.
Fil-kċina hemm friġġ kbir u mejda tonda. In the kitchen there is a big fridge and a round table.
Fis-salott hemm sufan griż. In the living room there is a grey sofa.
Jien naqra ħafna kotba. I read many books.
Ir-raġel tiegħi jħobb il-mużika. My husband likes music.
Filgħaxija nixorbu te flimkien. In the evening we drink tea together.
Fi tmiem il-ġimgħa nżuru lill-ħbieb. At the weekend we visit friends.
Aħna kuntenti ħafna b'din il-ħajja. We are very happy with this life.
Story 25
Jien, oħti u ommi noqogħdu flimkien. My sister, my mother, and I live together.
Aħna tliet nisa fid-dar. We are three women in the house.
Ommi taħdem ħafna. My mother works a lot.
Oħti tistudja l-universita. My sister studies at university.
Jien naħdem part-time. I work part-time.
Filgħodu kulħadd joħrog kmieni. In the morning everyone leaves early.
Filgħaxija niltaqgħu fil-kċina. In the evening we meet in the kitchen.
Nieklu u nitkellmu flimkien. We eat and talk together.
Xi drabi naraw programm fuq it-TV. Sometimes we watch a program on TV.
Id-dar tagħna hija kalma u sabiħa. Our home is calm and beautiful.
Story 26
Missieri għandu ħu u oħt. My father has a brother and a sister.
għalhekk għandi ziju u zija. So I have an uncle and an aunt.
Il-familja tagħna tiltaqa' ħafna drabi. Our family meets very often.
In-nanna tagħmel ħafna ikel. Grandmother makes a lot of food.
In-nannu jgħid stejjer antiki. Grandfather tells old stories.
Il-kuġini tiegħi jilagħbu fil-ġnien. My cousins play in the garden.
Aħna nieklu fuq mejda kbira. We eat at a big table.
Wara nixorbu kafè jew te. After that we drink coffee or tea.
Kulħadd jidher ferħan. Everyone looks happy.
Jiena nħobb dawn il-laqgħat tal-familja. I love these family gatherings.
Story 27
Jien student u naħdem ukoll. I am a student and I work too.
Filgħodu mmur l-iskola. In the morning I go to school.
Wara mmur għax-xogħol. After that I go to work.
Meta nasal id-dar inkun għajjien. When I arrive home I am tired.
Ommi tagħmilli te sħun. My mother makes hot tea for me.
Missieri jistaqsini dwar il-ġurnata tiegħi. My father asks me about my day.
Oħti tgħinni xi kultant. My sister helps me sometimes.
Filgħaxija nagħmel il-homework. In the evening I do homework.
Wara norqod kmieni. After that I sleep early.
Il-familja tiegħi tagħtini ħafna saħħa. My family gives me a lot of strength.
Story 28
Aħna noqogħdu f'dar b'żewġ sulari. We live in a two-floor house.
Fil-pjan t'isfel hemm il-kċina u s-salott. On the ground floor there is the kitchen and the living room.
Fil-pjan ta' fuq hemm il-kmamar tas-sodda. Upstairs there are the bedrooms.
Il-kamra tiegħi għandha tieqa kbira. My room has a big window.
ħemm skrivanija u siġġu ħdejn it-tieqa. There is a desk and a chair near the window.
Fil-kċina hemm friġġ u armarju. In the kitchen there is a fridge and a cupboard.
Fis-salott hemm sufan u mejda żgħira. In the living room there is a sofa and a small table.
Id-dar tagħna hija moderna. Our house is modern.
Aħna nżommuha nadifa kuljum. We keep it clean every day.
Jien inħobb ħafna fejn noqgħod. I like where I live very much.
Story 29
Jien u marti nħobbu nistiednu lill-ħbieb. My wife and I like inviting friends.
Filgħaxija nagħmlu ikla żgħira. In the evening we make a small meal.
Fuq il-mejda jkun hemm ħobż, ġobon u insalata. On the table there are bread, cheese, and salad.
Xi kultant nagħmlu pizza. Sometimes we make pizza.
Il-ħbieb tagħna jġibu kejk jew meraq. Our friends bring cake or juice.
Aħna noqogħdu fis-salott u nitkellmu. We sit in the living room and talk.
Xi drabi naraw film flimkien. Sometimes we watch a film together.
Id-dar tagħna ma hijiex kbira imma hija komda. Our home is not big but it is comfortable.
Il-ħbieb iħobbu jiġu għandna. Our friends like coming to our home.
Aħna nħobbu ħafna dawn il-lejliet. We like these evenings very much.
Story 30
Il-familja tiegħi tgħix ħajja sempliċi. My family lives a simple life.
Filgħodu nqumu kmieni. In the morning we wake up early.
Ommi tagħmel il-kafè u t-te. My mother makes the coffee and the tea.
Missieri jiftaħ it-tieqa fil-kċina. My father opens the window in the kitchen.
Jien u ħija nħejju għall-iskola. My brother and I get ready for school.
Wara nofsinhar nerġgħu lura d-dar. In the afternoon we come back home.
Nieklu flimkien fuq il-mejda. We eat together at the table.
Filgħaxija nitkellmu dwar il-ġurnata. In the evening we talk about the day.
Wara nistrieħu fis-salott. After that we rest in the living room.
Din il-ħajja tagħmilni kuntent ħafna. This life makes me very happy.
