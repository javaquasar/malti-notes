Ordering Patterns

Service

Reservation and Bill

Reservation

Nixtieq nagħmel riżervazzjoni.I would like to make a reservation.
Għal tnejn, jekk jogħġbok.For two, please.

Bill

Il-kont, jekk jogħġbok.The bill, please.
Nista' nħallas bil-karta?Can I pay by card?
Dialogues

Mini-dialogues

Ordering food

A: Il-menu, jekk jogħġbok.A: The menu, please.
B: Żgur. X'tixtieq tiekol?B: Of course. What would you like to eat?
A: Nixtieq pizza u ilma.A: I would like pizza and water.

Asking for the bill

A: Il-kont, jekk jogħġbok.A: The bill, please.
B: Żgur. Trid tħallas bil-karta jew bi flus kontanti?B: Of course. Would you like to pay by card or cash?
A: Bil-karta, jekk jogħġbok.A: By card, please.
Role Play

Role Play Ideas

Customer

Ask for the menu, order one dish and one drink, then ask for the bill.

Waiter / waitress

Offer the menu, ask what the customer would like, and explain how they can pay.

50 Thematic Phrases

Nista' nieħu te?Can I have tea?
Nista' nieħu meraq?Can I have juice?

Jekk jogħġbok, ġib ilma.Please bring water.
Nixtieq xi ħaġa ħafifa.I would like something light.
Nixtieq deżerta wkoll.I would also like dessert.
X'hemm fil-menu tal-lum?What is on today’s menu?
X'inhu l-platt tal-lum?What is the dish of the day?
Dan fih laħam?Does this contain meat?
Dan fih ġobon?Does this contain cheese?
Dan huwa pikkanti?Is this spicy?
Ma niekolx laħam.I do not eat meat.
Ma nixrobx alkoħol.I do not drink alcohol.
Għandi allerġija.I have an allergy.
Jekk jogħġbok, mingħajr ġobon.Please, without cheese.
Jekk jogħġbok, mingħajr zokkor.Please, without sugar.
Tixtieq deżerta?Would you like dessert?
Le, grazzi. Jien mimli.No, thank you. I am full.
Iva, nixtieq kejk.Yes, I would like cake.
Kemm jiswa dan il-platt?How much does this dish cost?
Dan jiswa għaxar ewro.This costs ten euro.

L-ikla t-tajba.Enjoy your meal.
Lilek ukoll.You too.
