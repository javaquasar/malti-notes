Core Verbs

Everyday Verbs for a Normal Day

Maltese English Example
inqum I get up Filgħodu inqum kmieni.
immur I go Immur ix-xogħol filgħodu.
naħdem I work Naħdem f'uffiċċju.
niekol I eat F'nofsinhar niekol soppa.
nixrob I drink Filgħodu nixrob kafè.
nistrieħ I rest Wara x-xogħol nistrieħ.
niltaqa' I meet Niltaqa' mal-ħbieb tiegħi.
noqgħod I live / stay Noqgħod Malta.
nimxi I walk Xi kultant nimxi d-dar.
norqod I sleep Bil-lejl norqod id-dar.
Time Blocks

Useful Time Phrases for Routine Answers

Day Parts

  • filgħodu - in the morning
  • f'nofsinhar - at midday / at lunch time
  • waranofsinhar - in the afternoon
  • filgħaxija - in the evening
  • bil-lejl - at night

Frequency

  • kuljum - every day
  • normalment - normally
  • spiss - often
  • xi kultant - sometimes
  • qatt - never
time + verb + place / detail
  • Filgħodu nixrob kafè d-dar.
  • Waranofsinhar niltaqa' ma' sħabi.
  • Filgħaxija niekol mal-familja.
  • Bil-lejl norqod kmieni.
Templates

Safe Sentence Templates

Simple Present

Jien + verb + time + place
  • Jien naħdem filgħodu.
  • Jien niekol id-dar.
  • Jien niltaqa' mal-ħbieb filgħaxija.

Present Continuous

qed / qiegħed / qiegħda + verb
  • Jien qiegħed naħdem.
  • Jien qiegħda nixrob te.
  • Aħna qegħdin nieklu.

Likes and Routine

inħobb + verb
  • Inħobb nixrob kafè.
  • Inħobb nimxi filgħaxija.
  • Ma nħobbx inqum kmieni.

After Work

wara + noun + verb
  • Wara x-xogħol immur id-dar.
  • Wara x-xogħol nistrieħ.
  • Wara x-xogħol niltaqa' ma' sħabi.
Speaking

Routine Questions and Short Answers

Common Questions

X'tagħmel filgħodu?
What do you do in the morning?
Fejn taħdem?
Where do you work?
X'tiekol f'nofsinhar?
What do you eat at midday?
X'tixrob filgħodu?
What do you drink in the morning?
X'tagħmel wara x-xogħol?
What do you do after work?

Safe Answers

Filgħodu nixrob kafè u niekol ftit ħobż.
In the morning I drink coffee and eat some bread.
Naħdem f'uffiċċju.
I work in an office.
F'nofsinhar niekol ikla ħafifa.
At midday I eat a light meal.
Wara x-xogħol immur id-dar u nistrieħ.
After work I go home and rest.
Xi kultant niltaqa' mal-ħbieb tiegħi.
Sometimes I meet my friends.

Model Story

10-Sentence Daily Routine Model

Kuljum inqum fis-sebgħa.
Every day I get up at seven.
Filgħodu nixrob kafè d-dar.
In the morning I drink coffee at home.
Xi drabi niekol ftit ħobż u ġobon.
Sometimes I eat some bread and cheese.
Wara immur ix-xogħol.
After that I go to work.
Naħdem f'uffiċċju.
I work in an office.
F'nofsinhar niekol ikla ħafifa.
At midday I eat a light meal.
Waranofsinhar inkompli naħdem.
In the afternoon I continue to work.
Wara x-xogħol immur id-dar.
After work I go home.
Filgħaxija niltaqa' mal-familja jew mal-ħbieb tiegħi.
In the evening I meet my family or my friends.
Bil-lejl nistrieħ u norqod.
At night I rest and sleep.

Use this story as a model. Change only one or two details at a time: time, transport, food, place, or who you meet.
