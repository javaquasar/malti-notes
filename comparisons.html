People

Comparing People

Huwa itwal minn ħuh.He is taller than his brother.
Hija iqsar minn oħtha.She is shorter than her sister.
Dan it-tifel huwa iżgħar minn dak it-tifel.This boy is smaller than that boy.
Dik il-mara tidher isbaħ.That woman looks more beautiful.
Homes

Comparing Homes and Places

Din id-dar akbar minn dik.This house is bigger than that one.
Il-kamra tiegħi iżgħar mill-kamra ta' ħija.My room is smaller than her room.
Valletta hija aktar storika minn belt żgħira.Valletta is more historic than a small town.
Dan il-post aħjar għall-mixjiet.This place is better for walks.
Objects

Comparing Objects

Din il-mejda akbar minn dik il-mejda.This table is bigger than that table.
Dan is-siġġu aktar komdu.This chair is more comfortable.
Dan il-flokk irħis aktar.This shirt is cheaper.
Din il-karozza aħjar minn dik.This car is better than that one.
Q&A

Questions and Answers

Questions

Liema dar akbar?Which house is bigger?
Min itwal?Who is taller?
Liema siġġu aktar komdu?Which chair is more comfortable?

Answers

Din id-dar akbar.This house is bigger.
Hija itwal.She is taller.
Dan is-siġġu aktar komdu.This chair is more comfortable.
Review

Wrong vs Right

Wrong

Din akbar minn.Sentence not complete.

Right

Din akbar minn dik.This is bigger than that.
Phrases

50 Thematic Phrases

Dan akbar minn dak.This is bigger than that.
Dan iżgħar minn dak.This is smaller than that.
Din isbaħ minn dik.This is more beautiful than that.
Dan aħjar minn dak.This is better than that.
Huwa itwal minn ħuh.He is taller than his brother.
Hija itwal minn oħtha.She is taller than her sister.
Huwa iqsar minn missieru.He is shorter than his father.
Hija iqsar minn ommha.She is shorter than her mother.
Dan it-tifel ikbar minn dak.This boy is bigger than that one.
Din it-tifla iżgħar minn dik.This girl is smaller than that one.
Din id-dar akbar minn dik.This house is bigger than that one.
Il-kamra tiegħi iżgħar minn tiegħek.My room is smaller than yours.
Din il-kamra isbaħ.This room is more beautiful.
Dan is-salott aktar komdu.This living room is more comfortable.
Dan is-siġġu aktar komdu minn dak.This chair is more comfortable than that one.
Din il-mejda akbar minn dik il-mejda.This table is bigger than that table.
Dan il-flokk irħis aktar.This shirt is cheaper.
Din il-libsa għalja aktar.This dress is more expensive.
Dawn iż-żraben aħjar minn dawk.These shoes are better than those.
Dan il-ktieb eħfef.This book is lighter.
Dak il-ktieb itqal.That book is heavier.
Il-karozza tiegħi akbar.My car is bigger.
Ir-rota tiegħi iżgħar.My bicycle is smaller.
Il-karozza aħjar għall-vjaġġ twil.The car is better for a long trip.
Ir-rota aħjar għall-belt.The bicycle is better for the city.
Dan il-kelb ikbar minn dak il-qattus.This dog is bigger than that cat.
Iż-żiemel akbar mill-kelb.The horse is bigger than the dog.
Il-fenek iżgħar mill-kelb.The rabbit is smaller than the dog.
Din il-ġiraffa itwal mill-iljun.This giraffe is taller than the lion.
Valletta aktar storika minn post modern.Valletta is more historic than a modern place.
Dan il-post isbaħ bil-lejl.This place is more beautiful at night.
Il-baħar aħjar fis-sajf.The sea is better in summer.
It-temp illum aħjar minn ilbieraħ.The weather today is better than yesterday.
Illum hemm aktar xemx.Today there is more sun.
Illum hemm inqas riħ.Today there is less wind.
Dan ir-restorant aħjar minn dak.This restaurant is better than that one.
Din il-pizza akbar minn dik.This pizza is bigger than that one.
Din is-soppa aktar sħuna.This soup is hotter.
Dan il-kafè aħjar.This coffee is better.
Din il-borża akbar mill-oħra.This bag is bigger than the other one.
Dan il-mobile aktar modern.This mobile phone is more modern.
Dak il-kompjuter aktar mgħaġġel.That computer is faster.
Din il-klass aktar kwieta.This class is quieter.
Dan l-eżami aktar diffiċli.This exam is more difficult.
Il-Malti aktar diffiċli mill-Ingliż?Is Maltese more difficult than English?
Le, għalija l-Malti aktar sabiħ.No, for me Maltese is more beautiful.
Din il-lezzjoni aktar faċli.This lesson is easier.
Din il-paġna aħjar għar-reviżjoni.This page is better for revision.
Dan l-eżempju aktar ċar.This example is clearer.
Din il-persuna aktar ferrieħa minn dik.This person is more cheerful than that one.
Dan l-istudent aktar kunfidenti.This student is more confident.
Jien aħjar illum minn ilbieraħ.I am better today than yesterday.
