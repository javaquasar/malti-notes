People

Comparing People

Huwa itwal minn ħuh.He is taller than his brother.
Hija iqsar minn oħtha.She is shorter than her sister.
Dan it-tifel huwa iżgħar minn dak it-tifel.This boy is smaller than that boy.
Dik il-mara tidher isbaħ.That woman looks more beautiful.
Homes

Comparing Homes and Places

Din id-dar akbar minn dik.This house is bigger than that one.
Il-kamra tiegħi iżgħar mill-kamra ta' ħija.My room is smaller than her room.
Valletta hija aktar storika minn belt żgħira.Valletta is more historic than a small town.
Dan il-post aħjar għall-mixjiet.This place is better for walks.
Objects

Comparing Objects

Din il-mejda akbar minn dik il-mejda.This table is bigger than that table.
Dan is-siġġu aktar komdu.This chair is more comfortable.
Dan il-flokk irħis aktar.This shirt is cheaper.
Din il-karozza aħjar minn dik.This car is better than that one.
