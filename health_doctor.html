Pain

I Have Pain

Għandi uġigħ ta' ras.I have a headache.
Għandi uġigħ fil-griżmejn.I have a sore throat.
Għandi uġigħ fiż-żaqq.I have a stomach ache.
Għandi deni.I have a fever.
Doctor

At the Doctor

Ma nħossnix tajjeb.I do not feel well.
Għandi bżonn tabib.I need a doctor.
Ili marid jumejn.I have been ill for two days.
Pharmacy

At the Pharmacy

Għandi bżonn mediċina.I need medicine.
Għandek xi ħaġa għas-sogħla?Do you have something for a cough?
Għandek xi ħaġa għall-uġigħ ta' ras?Do you have something for a headache?
Dialogues

Mini-dialogues

At the doctor

A: X'għandek?A: What is wrong?
B: Għandi uġigħ ta' ras u deni.B: I have a headache and a fever.
A: Trid tistrieħ.A: You need to rest.

At the pharmacy

A: Għandi sogħla.A: I have a cough.
B: Għandna mediċina għas-sogħla.B: We have medicine for cough.
A: Grazzi ħafna.A: Thank you very much.
Role Play

Role Play Ideas

Patient

Say what hurts, how long you have felt bad, and what help you need.

Doctor / pharmacist

Ask simple questions, suggest rest or medicine, and keep the language clear and calm.
