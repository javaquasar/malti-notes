Study Pages

Verbs Guide

Verb patterns, tenses, questions, negatives, possession, imperative, and the course-wide verb bank.

Numbers, Calendar and Time

Numbers, ordinal forms, days, months, seasons, time phrases, and many practical examples.

Family, Home and Food

Family members, rooms, furniture, food, drinks, and longer family stories for speaking practice.

Home, Rooms and Furniture

Outside parts of the house, rooms, furniture, visual vocabulary cards, and 100 practical examples.

Location words like on, under, next to, behind, in front of, and between, with home-based examples.

Directions and Places in Town

Ask the way, give simple directions, and talk about common town places in clear speaking models.

Ordering patterns, menu vocabulary, bill and reservation phrases, mini-dialogues, and survival expressions.

Shopping and Clothes

Clothing vocabulary, colours, sizes, prices, mini-dialogues, and 50 practical shopping phrases.

Health and At the Doctor

Body and symptom vocabulary, doctor and pharmacy phrases, role play, and 50 survival expressions.

Can, Must, Want and Need

Core modal-style patterns with nista', irrid, għandi bżonn, and nixtieq.

Body and Appearance

Body parts, hair, eyes, appearance adjectives, masculine and feminine agreement, and 50 description phrases.

Weather and Seasons

Weather vocabulary, seasons, small talk, daily plans, and 50 practical weather phrases.

Comparisons

Simple comparison language for people, homes, objects, places, and speaking practice.

Daily Problems

Lost keys, late buses, home problems, stress, tiredness, and 50 everyday survival phrases.

Pronouns and Possession

Pronouns, possessives, demonstratives, prepositions, particles, and large question-answer practice.

Picture Description

Picture scenes, model sentences, useful questions, and fast speaking support for oral tasks.

Daily Routine

Routine verbs, time blocks, question-answer patterns, and a 10-sentence model about a normal day.

Sentence Builder

Sentence formulas like subject + verb + place + time, hemm, qed, and se + verb.

Common Mistakes

Quick correction page for high-frequency Level 1 mistakes in speaking and homework.

Domestic and Wild Animals

Animals page with domestic and wild animal vocabulary, description patterns, and 100 short model sentences.

Review Cards

Save vocabulary in your browser and repeat it later as simple flashcards.

Places and Events

Interesting places in Malta, festivals, descriptive adjectives, and ready-made models for talking about visits and cultural events.

Transport and Travel

Transport vocabulary, travel patterns, and a full route example from Sliema to Valletta by ferry, bus, car, bicycle, and taxi.

The Collective

Fruit, vegetables, insects, food vocabulary, adjective agreement, shopping patterns, and collective noun practice.

Emotions

Emotion vocabulary, masculine and feminine forms, the verb inħossni, and ready-made speaking models.

Impactful People

Past tense, professions, adjectives, and short biography patterns for talking about important people.

Food Preferences

Food vocabulary, meals during the day, important food verbs, dialogue, and traditional Maltese dishes.
